The [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/quickstart) and [Guides](/docs/guides) sections cover everyday use.
These pages go deeper: how to reshape the HTTP transport, call endpoints that have no generated method, hook into
the request/response cycle, and reason about validation, logging, and concurrency.
